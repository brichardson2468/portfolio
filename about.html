About Me

After graduating Emory University with a degree in Neuroscience I worked in healthcare for 10 years, everything from a lab, to an EMT on an ambulance, to a geriatric psychiatric hospital unit. I then attended medical school before deciding to make the career switch to software engineering during the COVID-19 pandemic.

With my previous experience in healthcare and my new found passion for software, I hope to break into the health tech industry and help build the next generation of healthcare technology.
